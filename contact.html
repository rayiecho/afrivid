Quick Answers

How do I upgrade to Pro?
Email us at afrividstudio@gmail.com with subject "Upgrade to Pro". We'll set you up within 24 hours. M-Pesa and card payments accepted.
My video is stuck or not generating — what do I do?
Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R), clear your browser cache and try again. If it persists, email us with the topic you tried and your browser name.
Can I use AfriVid for my church / school / NGO?
Absolutely. We have special pricing for churches, schools and NGOs. Email us with your organization name and we'll work something out.
How do I delete my account?
Email us at afrividstudio@gmail.com with subject "Delete My Account". We'll process it within 48 hours and confirm when done.
Is AfriVid available in French or Swahili?
The interface is currently in English but AfriVid can create and translate content to French, Swahili, Yoruba, Hausa and Amharic. Full multilingual interface coming soon.
