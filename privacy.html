Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026 · Effective immediately

AfriVid Studio ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use AfriVid Studio ("the Service") at afrivid.studio.

🌍 AfriVid Studio is operated by AfriVid Studio, a technology initiative based in Kenya. By using our service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use AfriVid Studio:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Usage data — videos created, photos edited, designs made, features used
  • Content you create — video scripts, uploaded photos, design elements
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited
  • Payment information — handled securely by our payment partners, we do not store card details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the AfriVid Studio service
  • To track your usage limits (free vs Pro plan)
  • To send important service updates and announcements
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To prevent abuse and ensure fair usage
  • To analyze how features are used and improve them

3. AfriBrain — African AI Training Data

🧠 With your explicit consent, anonymized transcriptions from your videos may be used to help train AfriBrain — Africa's first multilingual AI model. This data is stripped of all personal identifiers before use. You can opt out at any time in your account settings. This is optional and does not affect your use of AfriVid Studio.

4. Third-Party Services

AfriVid Studio uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database storage
  • Cloudflare — hosting, image storage (R2) and AI processing
  • Google Text-to-Speech — voice generation for videos
  • Anthropic Claude — AI script generation and editing
  • OpenAI Whisper (via Cloudflare) — audio transcription

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Your account data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers
  • Videos and images are stored on Cloudflare R2 storage
  • We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties — ever
  • We do not display advertisements or allow advertisers to target you

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update your name or email in account settings
  • Deletion — request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out — withdraw consent for AfriBrain data training at any time
  • Portability — export your created content before deleting your account

7. Children's Privacy

AfriVid Studio is available to users aged 13 and above. Users under 18 must have parental or guardian consent to use the service. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us immediately.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our service grows. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by a prominent notice on the platform. Continued use of AfriVid Studio after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
