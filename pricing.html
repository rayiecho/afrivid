Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a dollar card to sign up?
No. The free plan requires no payment at all. For Pro, we are working on M-Pesa and MTN Mobile Money support. Currently you can pay via email arrangement.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes. Email us at afrividstudio@gmail.com and we'll cancel within 24 hours. No questions asked.
What languages does AfriVid support?
AfriVid creates videos in English and can translate to Swahili, French, Yoruba, Hausa, Amharic and Portuguese. More African languages coming soon.
Who owns the videos I create?
You do. 100%. AfriVid does not claim ownership of any content you produce on our platform.
Is there a student or NGO discount?
Yes. Email us with proof of student status or NGO registration and we'll discuss special pricing. We believe in making AI accessible to all Africans.
What happens when I hit my free limit?
You'll see a friendly upgrade prompt. Your existing work is never deleted. You can upgrade to Pro or wait until next month when limits reset.
